Solar Power Plant at Lovely Professional University (LPU)

Overview of the Solar Power Plant

The Solar Power Plant at Lovely Professional University (LPU) has been installed on the terrace of the 38-block building. This state-of-the-art installation features 400 solar panels designed to generate electricity efficiently.

The plant was built in 2013, and the installation process was completed in just three months. The system generates 600V of DC current and produces a power output of 100KW (0.1 MW) of electricity.

Environmental Impact and Solar Energy

Solar energy has a positive impact on the environment by reducing air pollution and mitigating water scarcity. At LPU, we are deeply committed to promoting sustainability, and the university has installed almost 1000 KW of solar panels across its lush-green campus.

These solar panels cater to the electricity needs of hundreds of young minds, helping to reduce the carbon footprint of the university. It is evident that when you visit the campus, almost every terrace of every building is covered with solar panels, making LPU a leading example of renewable energy adoption in India.

Key Facts About the Solar Power Plant

  • Location: Terrace of 38-block at LPU
  • Number of Solar Panels: 400
  • Power Generated: 100 KW (0.1 MW)
  • DC Current Generated: 600V
  • Year of Installation: 2013
  • Installation Time: 3 months
